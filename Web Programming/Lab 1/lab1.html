BACKSTORY

First of all, we should get accustomed Oscar's little story.

We adopted this little fellow about one year ago. It was a very impulsive decision and we would not expect how much of a change it would make in our lives, just having another living being roam around the house and follow us and play. At that time, I was not at home, so my parents had to go through all the different cat-owner phases:

  • showering him with toys that he likes
  • after a week he stops liking them
  • buying him more toys on christmas...
  • ...that he never ONCE plays with them
  • Him finding his one true passion in life: sleep!
  • Him finding about his other big interest: BITING AND SCRATCHING

The rest might as well be history. Even though owning a pet is always a challenge in responsibility and affection, we have no regrets in living with such a majestic creature
